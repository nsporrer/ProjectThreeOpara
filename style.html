He states the Margaret Thatcher contributed to his desire to become a designer, Thatcher took away school children's milk and Opara claimed to have loved milk and he wanted to be part of a movement that “gave back and collaborated for the common good.” He knows it's strange, but he says childhood impressions are very powerful and that moment really stuck with him and shaped his attitude.

During his academic studies he moved to Holland for one year, where he was blown away at how integrated their design was into their life. And his over style of work seems to be heavily influenced by the Dutch design style
