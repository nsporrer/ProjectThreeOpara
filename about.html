About

Eddie Opara was born in Wandsworth London in 1972

He attended 4 colleges in total, starting at Wimbledon College and Kingston College, he finished his Honors BA at the London College of Printing in 1995 and eventually received his master's degree after traveling to the United States and studying at Yale university... He received his MFA in 1997

He has worked at many different design studios. Including ATG, Imaginary Forces, 2x4 (senior art director), The Map Office (His own studio) 2005

In 2010 he joined the New York office at the design studio Pentagram where he still works today.
